The Concept

UX Design + Front End WordPress

Pacific Immigrant Resources Society needed a website redesign to help new female immigrants find programs best suited to integrate into Canadian culture. Some technical aspects was an intuitive navigation, easy and accessible information regarding their programs, integration of videos, and the ability for the organization to update the website themselves in CMS WordPress. The strategy in this project was UI organization and integrating their brand values throughout the website and telling their story through images.

Low Fidelity Prototype

The Wireframe

The reorganization of PIRS navigation and information system was done through a variety of one on one workshops and then restructured in the wireframe. Once the wireframe was approved the site design and development was able to move forward.

The Look & Feel

Style Guide

A visual style guide was produced stating all required visual hierarchy used throughout the website. The color palette was inspired by the PIRS brand colors used in their print collateral. The website feel needed to be personal, warm, and approachable while maintaining a sense of professionalism.
