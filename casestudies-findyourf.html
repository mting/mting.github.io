The Concept

Visual Design + Concept Development

FindYourF is a trans-media digital campaign based on the current FStandsFor campaign. This concept is based on a real world game where the Fendi followers finds clues hidden in a series of mediums involving print collateral, web URLs, art murals and social media. The purpose is to spread brand awareness and create hype within an elite subculture. The end goal is once all clues are collected followers gets an exclusive invitation to a grand fashion gala.

The Research

The Inspiration

This campaign was inspired by several alternate reality games using social media and digital spaces as it's omnichannel output. Jay-Z Decoded uses space and shows parts of the unreleased book so as a progression to the final launch. Rick and Morty uses instagram as a game to discover new worlds. Nine Inch Nails "Year Zero" campaign displays hidden clues at certain venues while releasing parts of music videos creating an alternate reality for fans to participate.

The Look & Feel

The Style Guide

The style of the campaign is very bright with the use of many different typography treatments. The result is creating a clash and a feeling of rebelliousness, and underground culture. The baselines and x-height of fonts are paired and rules are broken in order to convey a sense of dissonence.

The Digital Channels

The Invitation

The FindYourF is launched as a private invitation to the women’s wear fashion show. On the invitation a hint of FindYourF is placed on the invitation that leads to a web URL (findyourf.com) where the next clue is displayed.

The Website

At the women’s fashion show swag bags are given out where a scarf is found and it displays the web URL of FindYourF.com. On the website it is displayed as a call out to the subculture who wants to embark on this journey of finding the other clues.

Social Media

Social media Instagram account leads to another series of clues that displays latitude and longitude of specific locations. The locations are based in areas that have Fendi presence such as Hong Kong, LA, New York, Paris, and Rome. Followers are required to follow @FunBeingFreaks to get the specific meeting time for the next clue.
